About Initiative Tracker

I, the mighty Aravill, needed a simple little tool to use to track the initiative of my players and monsters during combat in DnD. I also wanted to learn to use NodeJS Electron and Bootstrap frameworks, Voila - creation of this app.

Right now this app will only support simple initiative tracking - a list of initiative that the user can cycle through (first element becomes last), addition new players/monsters to this list and giving them descriptions. If I ever find the time and will, I will add more quality-of-life features like tracking HP and actions.
